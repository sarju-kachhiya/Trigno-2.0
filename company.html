Our Journey, Tringo Pizza!

The Trigno Pizza journey began with a love for good food and a vision to bring the heart of New York to your plate. Our founders, passionate food enthusiasts, embarked on a mission to introduce the world to the legendary New York-style pizza, where the crust is thin, crispy, and adorned with the freshest, high-quality ingredients.

The New York-Style Experience

What sets Trigno Pizza apart is our unwavering commitment to authenticity. We understand that every slice of New York-style pizza should be a slice of nostalgia, a taste of the bustling streets of the Big Apple. Our dough is meticulously prepared, hand-tossed, and baked to perfection.

The Trigno Promise

Our promise is simple but profound: every dish is crafted with love. We pay meticulous attention to detail, from the way we knead our dough to the precise moment your pizza leaves our oven. We believe that when you dine with us, you're not just a customer; you're a guest, and every guest is a part of our Trigno family.
